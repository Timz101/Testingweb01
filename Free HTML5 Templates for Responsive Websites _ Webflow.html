Using Webflow’s free HTML5 templates

Just pick the right Webflow free HTML5 template for you or your business, then jump into the Designer to tweak colors, typography, layout, content—whatever. When you’re ready to publish, use Webflow Hosting to ensure your site loads fast and enjoys 99.9% uptime.

What's this CMS thing?

Design hundreds of pages–at once

Create a single template page (for, say, a blog post) and that design will automatically apply to every item in that collection (that is, all your blog posts).

Work with all kinds of content

Create custom collections to describe your unique content. Webflow lets you mix and match custom fields to build the perfect content structure.

Write and edit–right on the page

Make changes right on the live website and your content will update automatically, where it appears. Or just shoot a link to your client or team so they can take care of it.
